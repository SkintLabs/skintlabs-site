Privacy Policy

Effective April 2026  ·  Skint Labs, Melbourne, Australia

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Skint Labs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles data in connection with WonderwallAi — our open-source AI firewall SDK and hosted API. We are committed to transparency and to protecting the privacy of our users and their end users.

Data We Collect

When you use the WonderwallAi hosted API, we collect and process:

  • API requests: the text content submitted for scanning (e.g. user prompts, messages) to perform firewall analysis.
  • Usage metadata: request counts, timestamps, and scan results used for billing and rate limiting.
  • Account information: email address and API key details provided during registration.

If you use the open-source SDK self-hosted, no data is transmitted to us. All processing occurs on your own infrastructure.

How We Use Your Data

  • API request content is processed in real time to perform firewall scanning and is not stored beyond the duration of the request.
  • Usage metadata is retained for billing reconciliation and service improvement.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • We do not use your submitted content to train AI models.

SMS Marketing

With your explicit consent, Skint Labs may send you SMS messages for the following purposes:

  • Product updates and new feature announcements for WonderwallAi.
  • Reminders about your subscription, billing, or API key status.
  • Promotional offers relevant to your use of WonderwallAi.

Your mobile number is never sold or shared with third parties for their marketing. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS we send, or by emailing info@skintlabs.ai with the subject line "SMS Opt Out".

Data Retention

API request content is not persistently stored. Usage metadata and account data are retained for the duration of your account and for 90 days following account closure, after which all data is permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

  • Railway: Cloud hosting for the WonderwallAi hosted API. Railway Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for paid API plans. Stripe Privacy Policy.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), secure API key management, and access controls. The WonderwallAi SDK is open source and MIT licensed — you are welcome to audit the code at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email. Your continued use of WonderwallAi after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
