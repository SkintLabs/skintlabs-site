About

SKINT LABS

Skint Labs provides high-performance AI security and automation from our base in Australia. We develop focused products designed to bridge the gap between experimental language models and enterprise-grade execution.

Our current ecosystem features Jerry, an autonomous customer service platform for Shopify, and WonderwallAi, an open-source LLM firewall SDK. WonderwallAi secures deployments by blocking prompt injection, data exfiltration, and off-topic abuse in under 2ms, ensuring that every interaction remains profitable and secure.

As the industry moves beyond hype, audibility has become the primary requirement for professional AI deployment. If an agent's strategy cannot be explained, it is a liability. We have architected our products so that every decision point—from intent classification to security verdicts—is captured in a searchable, structured format. In modern AI development, transparency is the only sustainable moat.
