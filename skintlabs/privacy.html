Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Skint Labs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles personal information across our products and website. Skint Labs builds AI security and automation software including Jerry (AI customer service bot) and WonderwallAi (AI firewall SDK).

Information We Collect

  • Contact information: name, email address, and mobile number when you sign up for updates, trials, or our products.
  • Usage data: how you interact with our websites and products, used to improve our services.
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe — we do not store card details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our products and services.
  • To send product updates, announcements, and relevant promotional offers (with your consent).
  • To send SMS messages for reminders, updates, and promotions (with your explicit consent — see SMS Marketing below).
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

SMS Marketing

With your explicit consent, Skint Labs may send you SMS messages for the following purposes:

  • Updates and announcements about Skint Labs products (Jerry, WonderwallAi, and future products).
  • Reminders about your account, subscription, or billing status.
  • Promotional offers, early-bird deals, and launch notifications.

Your mobile number is never sold or shared with third parties for their marketing. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS we send, or by emailing info@skintlabs.ai with the subject line "SMS Opt Out".

Email Marketing

If you subscribe to our mailing list, we will send you product updates and promotional content. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@skintlabs.ai.
