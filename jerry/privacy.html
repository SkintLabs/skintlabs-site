Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026  ·  Skint Labs, Melbourne, Australia

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Skint Labs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles data when merchants and their customers use Jerry, our AI-powered customer service bot for Shopify. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices and protecting the privacy of everyone who interacts with Jerry.

Data Jerry Collects

When a merchant installs Jerry on their Shopify store, we collect and process the following categories of data:

  • Product catalog: product titles, descriptions, prices, variants, and inventory data from the merchant's Shopify store.
  • Order data: order identifiers, line items, and fulfilment status, used to answer order-related customer queries.
  • Customer queries: the text messages that shoppers send to the Jerry chat widget.
  • Chat sessions: conversation history within a session, used to maintain context and provide coherent responses.

How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used exclusively to power Jerry's AI customer service functionality:

  • Product and order data are indexed so Jerry can answer shopper questions accurately and in real time.
  • Chat session data is used to maintain conversational context within a single support interaction.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share merchant or customer data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • We do not use your data to train general-purpose AI models without explicit consent.

Shopify Data Accessed

Jerry accesses the following data via the Shopify API under the permissions you grant during installation:

  • Products (read_products, write_products): To sync and index your product catalog.
  • Orders (read_orders): To look up order status and details for customer inquiries. Jerry does not modify orders.
  • Customers (read_customers): To verify customer identity when handling order-related queries. Jerry does not modify customer records.

Data Retention

When you uninstall Jerry from your Shopify store, your store's data (product catalog, order data, and chat history) is retained for 90 days following the uninstall date. This retention period allows for reactivation without loss of your indexed data.

After 90 days, all data associated with your store is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems, including all backups.

Merchant Data Deletion Request

You may request immediate deletion of all your store's data at any time, before the 90-day retention period expires, by emailing us at info@skintlabs.ai with the subject line "Jerry Data Deletion Request" and your Shopify store URL.

We will process deletion requests within 5 business days and send confirmation once complete.

Third-Party Services

Jerry relies on the following third-party infrastructure providers to operate. Each provider has their own privacy policy governing their data handling practices:

  • Groq: Large language model (LLM) inference provider. Customer queries are processed through Groq's API to generate responses. Groq Privacy Policy.
  • Pinecone: Vector database used for semantic search over your product catalog. Pinecone Privacy Policy.
  • Railway: Cloud hosting provider where Jerry's backend is deployed. Railway Privacy Policy.

We select providers with strong privacy and security practices and share only the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), secure API key management, and access controls. Our infrastructure is hosted on Railway with automatic security updates.

SMS Marketing

With your explicit consent, Skint Labs may send you SMS messages for the following purposes:

  • Product updates and new feature announcements for Jerry.
  • Reminders about your subscription, billing, or account status.
  • Promotional offers and early-bird deals relevant to your use of Jerry.

We collect your mobile number solely for the above purposes. Your number is never sold or shared with third parties for their marketing. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS we send, or by emailing info@skintlabs.ai with the subject line "SMS Opt Out".

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated to active merchants via email. Your continued use of Jerry after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
